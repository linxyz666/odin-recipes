Vege Bolognese

Description

Vege Bolognese is a mouthwateringly good comfort food for chilly fall evenings or nightly munchees.

Ingredients:

  • Textured soy protein granules: 4dl
  • Spaghetti:
  • Stock cubes: 2-3: beef, chicken or veggie, as per your choice
  • Tomato pure: 0.5dl
  • Chopped tomatoes: 500g
  • Syrup or honey: 3tbs
  • Cooking cream:2 1/2dl
  • BBQ sauce:5tbs
  • Smoked paprika powder: 1 1/2tbs
  • Black pepper: 2ts
  • Ketchup:3tbs
  • Worchester Sauce: 3 shakes
  • Parmesan:for finnish and for taste
  • Water: 3dl

Steps:

  1. Cook soy protein for 5 min with a stock cube, then stir in the tomatoes, tomato pure and rest of the stock cubes.
  2. Add seasonings, souces and sugar and salt together with the cooking cream and let shimmer.
  3. Cook spaghetti and drain it of water.
  4. Cook the sauce for 20 minutes on low temprature, add water and seasonings if needed.
  5. Asseble on plate, add Parmesan and Felix Ketchup for added taste.
