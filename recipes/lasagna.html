Laconic Lasagna

Description

Laconic Lasagna is your go-to easy lasagna recipe that takes no more than 90 minutes to prepare, and feeds your whole family.

Ingredients:

  • Beef: 500g
  • Spaghetti sauce: 10dl
  • Cheese: 750g: You can use a mix of your own choice with mozzarella, cheddar or Monterey Jack, as well as Parmesan for added flavour.
  • Eggs: 2 large eggs
  • Seasoning: 2tb fresh or dried parsley, salt, and balck pepper to taste
  • Lasagna noodles: 9
  • Water: 3dl

Steps:

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
